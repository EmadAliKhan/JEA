Our School

Jamila Educational Academy

In the heart of our community, Jamila Educational Academy stands as a bastion of educational excellence, catering to the developmental needs of both preschoolers and primary school students. Our commitment to the Montessori philosophy has set us apart as an institution dedicated to laying the strongest foundations for young minds.At the core of our approach is the belief that every child is a unique individual, and learning should be as well. In our carefully prepared classrooms, children explore, experiment, and engage with specially designed materials that stimulate their curiosity and foster independence. Guided by experienced Montessori educators, students are empowered to take ownership of their learning journey, promoting self-motivation and critical thinking.We understand the crucial role the early years play in shaping a child's future.

Founder

Mansoor Ali Khan (LATE)

Mansoor Ali Khan, the visionary founder of Jamila Educational Academy, is a true beacon of educational empowerment and community development. His unwavering commitment to providing quality education and creating opportunities for the youth has had a profound impact on countless lives. Mansoor Ali Khan's journey is a testament to his passion and dedication. He saw the potential in education as a means to uplift individuals and communities, and he transformed that vision into a reality through the establishment of Jamila Educational Academy. Under his leadership, the academy has become a symbol of excellence and a place where students are nurtured to reach their full potential.Mansoor Ali Khan's leadership is marked by innovation and a forward-thinking approach. He has not only created a strong academic foundation but also fostered an environment that encourages creativity, critical thinking, and a sense of social responsibility. Through his tireless efforts, he has empowered students to dream big and achieve their goals.Beyond the walls of the academy, Mansoor Ali Khan is known for his philanthropic endeavors and commitment to community development. He understands that education is not just about classrooms and textbooks but also about making a positive impact on society. His dedication to the betterment of the community serves as an inspiration to all who have the privilege of knowing him.

Notice of Nondiscriminatory Policy As To Students

The JAMILA EDUCATONAL ACADEMY admits students of any race, color, national and ethnic origin to all the rights, privileges, programs, and activities generally accorded or made available to students at the school. It does not discriminate on the basis of race, color, national and ethnic origin in administration of its educational policies, admissions policies, scholarship and loan programs, and athletic and other school-administered programs.
